Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

NichiNichi ("the App") is a Japanese vocabulary flashcard application. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

NichiNichi does not collect any personal data. The App operates entirely on your device with no server communication.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device only using your browser's or app's local storage:

  • Your daily vocabulary study progress
  • Quiz history and scores
  • Study streak information
  • Your daily word goal setting

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties. There are no analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking libraries in the App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs.

5. Children's Privacy

NichiNichi is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children or anyone else, as the App collects no information at all.

6. Changes to This Policy

If we update this Privacy Policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
